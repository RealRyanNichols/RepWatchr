## Election Cycle

Harleton ISD holds trustee elections in **November** on the uniform election date, not in May like most Texas ISDs. Four-year staggered terms.

- **Nov 2026 ballot**: Place 3 (Fox), Place 4 (Evers), Place 7 (Muehlstein)
- **Nov 2028 ballot**: Place 1 (Wilson), Place 2 (Skaggs), Place 5 (Fitzgerald), Place 6 (McGill)

## Recent History

### November 2024: Major Turnover
Three new trustees elected in November 2024: Ben Wilson (Place 1), Tim Skaggs (Place 2), Brian Fitzgerald (Place 5). This represented significant board turnover, with nearly half the board changing in a single cycle.

### February 2024: Evers Appointment
Kevin Evers was appointed (not elected) to Place 4 in February 2024 to fill a vacancy. He will face voters for the first time in November 2026.

### November 2018: Muehlstein 3-Way Race and Bond Failure
Jacob Muehlstein won a three-way race for Place 7 with 44.6% of the vote (475 votes), unseating incumbent Billy Ridgeway. Harvey Fox won Place 3 with 93% over write-in candidate Wendy Denton. A $10.5 million bond proposition failed on the same ballot.

### 2018: Coleman Superintendent Saga
The board navigated a contentious superintendent situation involving Superintendent Coleman in 2018. Patrick McGill was quoted in the Longview News-Journal regarding the matter. Details of the resolution require further research.
