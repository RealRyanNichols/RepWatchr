## Notable District Context

### School Guardian Program (2018)
Hallsville ISD adopted a school guardian program in 2018, authorizing armed administrators on campuses. The board approved the measure on a 6-1 vote.

### Own Police Department
The district operates its own police department under Chief Chris Miller. **NOTE**: Josh Miller is running for Place 2; check for family tie to Chief Chris Miller.

### HB 1481 Cell Phone Ban
The district is in compliance with HB 1481 cell phone ban requirements.

### Teacher Compensation (August 2025)
In August 2025, the board approved a teacher compensation package providing raises of $1,000 to $5,000.
